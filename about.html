About Mini Game Hub

What is Mini Game Hub?

Mini Game Hub is a curated collection of free online games that you can play directly in your browser. No downloads, no sign-ups, no payments — just click and play.

Our Game Categories

We hand-pick games across 8 categories: Puzzle, Action, Arcade, Strategy, Sports, Girls, Board, and Merge. Every game on our site has been tested to ensure it loads fast and plays well on both desktop and mobile.

How It Works

All games are HTML5-based, meaning they run in any modern browser. We partner with game developers and platforms to bring you the best free gaming experience online.

For Game Developers

Want your game featured on Mini Game Hub? We're always looking for quality HTML5 games to add to our collection. Reach out to us at hello@mini-game-hub.vercel.app.
